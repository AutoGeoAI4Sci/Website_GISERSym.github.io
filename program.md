# Program (Planned Sessions)

Below is the current list of planned sessions for the GISER symposium. Session details (format, speakers, and timing) will be updated as they are confirmed.

## Planned Sessions

- **Sensing Human Experience with Emerging Geospatial Methods I** (Led by Dr. Jiaxin Feng)  
- **Sensing Human Experience with Emerging Geospatial Methods II** (Led by Dr. Yuhao Kang)  
- **Ethics in GIScience** (Led by Dr. Hongyu Zhang)  
- **Ethics in GIS Education** (Led by Dr. Yue Lin)  
- **Digital Twin and 3D Cities for Urban Analytics** (Led by Dr. Debayan Mandal and Jasper Li)  
- **Social Sensing for Disasters and Health Crises** (Led by Dr. Hoeyun Kwon)  
- **GIScience for Environmental Health** (Led by Joynal Abedin and Dr. Lei Zou)  
- **Sensing Urban Mobility: Human-Centered Learning, Prediction, and Decision-Making** (Led by Armita Kar)  
- **Past, Present, and Future of GIScience (Panel)** (Led by GISS)

## How to Propose a Session

Please send your session proposal to any organizing committee member by **October 30, 2025**.  
If you are unsure whom to contact, email the co-leads (Lei Zou and Yuhao Kang).
